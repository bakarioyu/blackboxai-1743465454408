About MWALIMU ONLINE

Empowering learners and educators worldwide with quality resources

Our Story

MWALIMU ONLINE was founded in 2020 with a simple mission: to make quality educational resources accessible to students and teachers across all curricula and education systems worldwide.

What began as a small collection of notes for Kenyan students has grown into a comprehensive platform serving learners from primary school through university preparation, covering multiple international education systems.

Education is the most powerful weapon which you can use to change the world. We're just making sure everyone has access to that weapon.

Our Mission & Values
